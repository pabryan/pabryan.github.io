Exams

Details

There will be two midterm exams and one final exam. You may bring one 8.5 by 11 inch handwritten sheet of notes (written on both sides) with you to the each midterm exam, and two sheets (written on both sides) to the final. No other materials are permitted. No electronic devices whatsoever are allowed.

The midterm exams will be held during class time in the lecture hall. The final exam will be in the lecture hall. The schedule may be found at http://blink.ucsd.edu/instructors/academic-info/exams/schedule.html#spring.
