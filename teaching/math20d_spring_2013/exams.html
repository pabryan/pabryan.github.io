Exams

Details

There will be two midterm exams and one final exam. You may bring one 8.5 by 11 inch handwritten sheet of notes - written on both sides - with you to the each midterm exam and two sheets - written on both sides - to the final. No other materials are permitted. See the calendar for dates.

The midterm exams will be held during class time. The final exam will be in the lecture room, and the schedule may be found at http://blink.ucsd.edu/instructors/academic-info/exams/schedule.html#spring.

Practice Midterm 1

See http://math.ucsd.edu/~abowers/20d/exams.html for a sample practice exam. We will not have a question on interest rates, but otherwise the midterm will be quite similar.
