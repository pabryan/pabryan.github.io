AMSI Winter School 2018: Comparison Geometry

These notes are very preliminary and subject to change considerably without warning!

These are lectures notes for the 2018 AMSI Winter School on curvature. They comprise a brief introduction to comparison geometry in five lectures. Lecture 00 contains background reading in preparation for the course. Students should be more or less familiar with the material discussed there but perhaps not necessarily my idiosyncrasies and approach to differential geometry. Thus it would be a good idea to read Lecture 00 first if only to become accustomed to my style.
