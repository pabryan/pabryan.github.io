MATH 142A Introduction to Analysis II

Welcome to Math142B. Here you will find course information such as the course schedule and homework along with any updates and announcements (so check regularly). This course follows on from where Math142A left off. We will look at integration theory, approximation by polynomials and sequences and series of functions.
