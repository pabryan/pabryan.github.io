Syllabus

Catalog Description: Differential geometry of curves and surfaces. Gauss and mean curvatures, geodesics, parallel displacement, Gauss-Bonnet theorem.

Textbook: Differential Geometry of Curves and Surfaces by Manfredo P. Do Carmo; published by Prentice Hall, Inc. 1978.

Subject Material: Chapters 1 - 4.

Homework: There will be regular homework exercises, due in the drop-off box in AP&M by 5pm on the due date. I strongly encourage you to discuss the homework with other students in the class and to attend the sections. You must write up the solutions yourself and mention any sources used and students with whom you collaborated. See UCSD's policy on Academic Integrity.

Note: some of the homework exercises are marked with a * in do Carmo which means that solutions are available at the back of the book. Please do not turn in copies of the solution. You must write up your solution in your own words, perhaps also providing a few more details; the solutions in the book are rather brief. I also strongly encourage you to attempt the exercies before consulting the solutions as you will understand them better this way and be better prepared for the exams which after all, constitute the major part of your grade.

Exams: There will be one midterm exam and one final exam. No materials are permitted. See the calendar for dates.

Grades: Homework: 30%. Midterm: 20%. Final: 50%.
